Helping companies since 2011

During summer 2011 our founder, Georgiana Gligor, decided to return from rainy Amsterdam to sunny Alba Iulia. Here she decided to start a company centered around solving customer's needs, using modern technologies, on a budget.

Today we are solving business problems with modern Artificial Intelligence tools and techniques.

The team has since grown to 6 full-time members, dedicated to learning and solving complex problems. We love open-source, so the products we create are always stable, high-performing, and with a short time-to-market.

Our small, well-glued, agile team, is always eager to discover the next challenge.

Organisers of the first Romanian conference dedicated to PHP
